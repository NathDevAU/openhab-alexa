Controlla il tuo sistema OpenHAB 2 con Alexa e myopenHAB.org

Descrizione dettagliata

La skill openHAB Alexa Smart Home ti permette di controllare luci, termostati e altri oggetti connessi ad openHAB 2 attraverso il servizio cloud myopenHAB.org. 

Il progetto open Home Automation Bus (openHAB) ha l'obiettivo di fornire una piattaforma universale di integrazione per qualunque oggetto dedicato alla domotica. It is designed to be absolutely vendor-neutral as well as hardware/protocol-agnostic. openHAB brings together different bus systems, hardware devices and interface protocols. Supported technologies include KNX, Modbus, DMX, Philips Hue, PLCBus, Sonos, Plugwise, IHC/Elko, RFXCOM, Homematic, Koubachi, Pulseaudio, Samsung TV, Bluetooth, One-Wire, Novelan, Asterisk, generic serial devices and many others. You will find openHAB at http://www.openhab.org/
